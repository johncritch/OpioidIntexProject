FAQs
What are the benefits to prescribing opioids?
Prescription opioids can be used to help relieve moderate-to-severe pain and are often prescribed following a surgery or injury, or for certain health conditions.

What are the negative side effects to prescribing opioids?
Besides the common side effects, opioids also cause physical dependence, possible addiciton, and overdose.
What are the alternative options to opioids?
Nonsteriodal anti-inflammatories, acetaminophen, and non-opioid prescription medications are available.

Do prescribers benefit from prescribing opioids?
The more opioids a doctor prescribes, the more money he or she gets paid by those same manufacturers, according to a new analysis from Harvard.
